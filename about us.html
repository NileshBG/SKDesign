About SK Design Studio

SK Design Studio is a leading architecture and interior design firm committed to shaping spaces that inspire, uplift, and function beautifully. Based in Maharashtra, we specialize in delivering innovative and sustainable design solutions for residential, commercial, and institutional projects.

Founded by a team of passionate architects and designers, our mission is to blend creativity with functionality — ensuring each space we design tells a story of elegance, purpose, and personality. With years of experience, modern tools, and a human-centered approach, we’ve transformed hundreds of ideas into real, liveable masterpieces.

Whether you need a full-scale architectural design, interior planning, or 3D visualization, SK Design Studio is your trusted partner in crafting the future of your space.
